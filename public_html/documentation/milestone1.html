Purpose, Audience, Goals

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to showcase what I have learned and can do throughout this 10 week boot camp. This will be a personal portfolio that will show who I am and what I have done.

Audience

This website will be directed towards professionals in the field of I.T. and the the like. My target audience will be recruiters, peers, and business managers.

Goals

My primary goal with this website is to find employment through the work I put into the website itself and through the work portfolio I will have on the site that will showcase what I have done.
